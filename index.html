Royal - Alexandria's Premier Cellar

As the premier alcohol retail monopoly in the historical city of Alexandria, Royal stands as a distinguished beacon of luxury, heritage, and refined taste. Situated prominently in the vibrant district of As Soyouf Bahri, Qesm Al Montazah, our custom-designed showroom hosts an unrivaled curation of elite international and national products. From the cellars of prestigious old-world vineyards to modern, hand-crafted micro-distilleries, we ensure that every bottle resting on our shelves meets the highest gold standard of distinction.

Our commitment to serving the discerning communities of Qesm Al Montazah and the wider Alexandria Governorate goes far beyond just providing an extensive inventory. We believe in crafting an educational journey, offering curated pairings, and delivering exemplary client services. Our temperature-controlled vaults preserve the exact aromatic integrity of our fine reserve red wines, aged single-malt scotch whiskies, smooth craft beers, and ready-to-drink premixes, ensuring they reach your glass in pristine condition.
