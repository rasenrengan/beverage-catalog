The Royal Sapphire Spritz

⏱️ 5 Mins 🍹 Easy 🥂 Spritz Glass

Ingredients

  • 50ml Premium Gin (e.g. Harry's Premium Gin)
  • 15ml Blue Curaçao liqueur
  • 120ml Premium Tonic Water
  • Fresh blueberries & lemon peel
  • Fresh rosemary sprig (garnish)

Preparation

  1. Fill a large balloon glass to the brim with crushed premium ice blocks.
  2. Pour the dry gin and blue curaçao over the ice, stirring gently.
  3. Top slowly with chilled tonic water to preserve carbonation.
  4. Express the lemon peel over the glass, drop in a few fresh blueberries.
  5. Light the tip of the rosemary briefly for a smoky aroma, and garnish.
Bold & Smoky

Smoked Rosemary Old Fashioned

⏱️ 7 Mins 🥃 Medium 🥃 Rocks Glass

Ingredients

  • 60ml Blended Scotch (e.g. Devlin Blended Whiskey)
  • 2 dashes Aromatic Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Premium brown sugar cube
  • Fresh rosemary sprig & orange peel
  • 1 Large clear ice block

Preparation

  1. Place brown sugar cube in rocks glass. Add bitters and muddle until dissolved.
  2. Use a kitchen torch to lightly char a fresh rosemary sprig until aromatic smoke releases inside the glass.
  3. Add a single large ice block on top of the rosemary.
  4. Pour the blended whiskey slowly, stirring continuously for 20 seconds.
  5. Express and garnish with an orange peel twist.
Exotic & Fruity

Alexandria Sunset Spritz

⏱️ 6 Mins 🍸 Easy 🍷 Wine Glass

Ingredients

  • 45ml Botanical Dry Gin (or Swan Premium Vodka)
  • 30ml Cold brewed organic hibiscus tea
  • 15ml Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 60ml Sparkling Wine (e.g. Aida Valmont)
  • Fresh mint leaves & lime wheel

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker with ice, combine the gin, hibiscus tea, and lime juice.
  2. Shake vigorously for 10 seconds until chilled.
  3. Double strain into a wine glass filled with fresh ice.
  4. Top with sparkling wine to create a gorgeous layered sunset look. Garnish with lime & mint.

The Sakara Golden Shandy

⏱️ 4 Mins 🍺 Easy 🍺 Pint Glass

Ingredients

  • 150ml Sakara Gold Beer (chilled)
  • 100ml Premium Lemonade
  • 15ml Homemade Ginger Syrup
  • Fresh mint sprig & lemon wheel

Preparation

  1. Stir the ginger syrup and cloudy lemonade together at the bottom of a cold pint glass.
  2. Add ice blocks if desired, then pour the chilled Sakara Gold slowly to prevent excess foam.
  3. Stir once very gently with a bar spoon.
  4. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a fresh sprig of Egyptian mint.

Stella Mint Cooler

Ingredients

  • 180ml Stella Lager (ice cold)
  • 30ml Fresh lime juice
  • 15ml Simple syrup

Preparation

Silky Connoisseur

The Devlin Sour

⏱️ 8 Mins 🥃 Hard 🍸 Coupe Glass

Ingredients

  • 60ml Devlin Blended Whiskey
  • 30ml Fresh lemon juice
  • 20ml Rich simple syrup (2:1 ratio)
  • 15ml Fresh egg white (or aquafaba)
  • 3 drops Angostura Bitters

Preparation

  1. Combine Devlin Whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white in a shaker without ice. Dry shake for 15 seconds to emulsify egg white.
  2. Add ice blocks and wet shake vigorously for another 12 seconds.
  3. Double strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. Drip Angostura bitters onto the velvety foam, swirling with a toothpick for art.

Cubana Mojito Smooth

⏱️ 6 Mins 🍹 Medium 🍹 Highball Glass

Ingredients

  • 60ml Cubana Rum Smooth
  • 8-10 Fresh Egyptian mint leaves
  • 1/2 Fresh lime cut into 4 wedges
  • 2 tsp Demerara brown sugar
  • Soda water to top

Preparation

  1. Muddle brown sugar and fresh lime wedges at the bottom of the glass to release juice.
  2. Add mint leaves, slapping them first, and muddle very gently to avoid bitterness.
  3. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  4. Pour the Cubana Rum Smooth over the top, top with soda, and stir fully from bottom.

Royal Golden Daiquiri

⏱️ 5 Mins 🍸 Easy 🍸 Coupe Glass

Ingredients

  • 60ml Cubana Rum Gold
  • 30ml Fresh lime juice
  • 15ml Rich cane sugar syrup
  • Dehydrated lime wheel (garnish)

Preparation

  1. Chill a coupe glass in the freezer.

Ingredients

  • 25ml Triple Sec (orange liqueur)

Preparation

Royal Espresso Martini

⏱️ 6 Mins ☕ Medium 🍸 Coupe Glass

Ingredients

  • 50ml Swan Premium Vodka
  • 25ml Rich Coffee Liqueur
  • 30ml Fresh hot espresso shot
  • 5ml Salted caramel syrup
  • 3 Espresso beans for floating

Preparation

  1. Brew a fresh espresso shot and let cool slightly.

Classic Dry Martini

⏱️ 5 Mins 🍸 Medium 🍸 Martini Glass

Ingredients

  • 60ml Butler's Gin (or Mosaic Gin)
  • 10ml Dry Vermouth
  • 1 large green olive (stuffed)

Preparation

Rich & Full-Bodied

Vintage Red Wines

Red wines derive their robust structure, deep ruby colors, and complex tannins from fermenting together with the dark grape skins. Standard varieties like Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, and Syrah (e.g., our esteemed Chateau de Grandville, Grand Marquis, and Beausoleil Merlot) exhibit rich flavors of dark cherries, oak, and spices. Select a fine red wine for premium, intimate dinners, celebration events, and cooler evenings.

Sommelier Recommended Pairings

Pairs outstandingly with char-grilled ribeye steak, roasted lamb chops with fresh herbs, smoked beef sliders, rich tomato pasta, gourmet dark chocolates, and roasted salted cashews or smoked almonds from the Royal Roastery.

Crisp & Refreshing

Crisp White Wines

White wines are fermented solely with pressed juice, bypassing skin contact to preserve a vibrant, bright yellow hues, crisp acidity, and refreshing floral or citrus notes. Selections like Chardonnay and Pinot Blanc (e.g. Cape Bay Chardonnay or Grand Marquis Pinot Blanc) bring alive hints of apples, lemons, and minerals. Perfect for hot beach days, sun-drenched lunches, or a crisp evening wind on the Alexandria coast.

Sommelier Recommended Pairings

Best enjoyed alongside freshly caught Mediterranean grilled sea bass, garlic lemon butter prawns, baked salmon, fresh green salads, goat cheese platters, salted almonds, and lightly toasted pita crackers.

Vibrant & Playful

Vibrant Rosé Wines

Rosé wines capture the best of both worlds through a brief, controlled maceration with grape skins (usually only a few hours) to achieve a beautiful pink gradient. Offering a playful balance of red berry fruitiness and clean, refreshing acidity, bottles like Obelisk Rosetta Rose and Omar Khayyam Rose are exceptionally versatile, easy-drinking, and elegant. Select a rosé wine to add a vibrant spark to social gatherings and sunsets.

Sommelier Recommended Pairings

Complements light Mediterranean tapas, prosciutto, smoked salmon rolls, grilled chicken skewers, fresh strawberries, feta and watermelon salads, salted roasted pistachios, and gourmet white chocolate truffles.
